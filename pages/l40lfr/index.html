The Valley Metro L40LFRs

The Valley Metro New Flyer L40LFRs were an order of 7 busses with fleet numbers 6669-6675. These busses were built in 2010 as 2009 models and were the only order of L40LFRs ever made. A staple of the FLASH route in their later years, they were all retired by mid-July 2024. By then, they were the oldest busses in active service the entire Valley Metro fleet and the final busses to feature the old purple and teal Valley Metro livery (except for 6669). Here is a photo album of these busses, taken from August 2023 to May 2024, within their final year of service.
